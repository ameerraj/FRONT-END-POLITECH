** Use this to direct your LLM to build the dashboard

What data it collects
It pulls several types of parliamentary activity straight from TBMM's website:

Law proposals (kanun teklifleri)
Votes / roll calls (oylamalar)
Written questions to ministers (yazılı sorular)
General debate motions and research motions/commissions (araştırma önergeleri/komisyonları)
Floor speeches from the official transcripts (genel kurul konuşmaları)
